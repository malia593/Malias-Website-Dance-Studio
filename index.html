ABOUT US

Welcome to MP Urban Dance Studio, where rhythm meets passion and creativity comes alive! Founded in 2010, our studio was born from a shared love of hip hop culture and a commitment to empowering dancers of all levels. We are more than just a dance studio—we are a movement, a family, and a space where individuality is celebrated through the power of dance.

Over the years, we've trained hundreds of dancers, competed in national and international competitions, and hosted unforgettable showcases that spotlight our talented students and instructors.

We have have passionate teachers that are professionals with years of performance and teaching experience. Out community and culture is amazing! We are a supportive and inclusive family where dancers of all backgrounds can thrive. We do from local showcases to global dance battles, we provide opportunities to shine on stage. We encourage dancers to embrace their unique style and push the boundaries of their creativity.

Join us at MP Urban Dance Studio and experience the heartbeat of hip hop through dance, music, and community. Let's create, inspire, and move together! "Dance is freedom. Dance is life. Welcome to the family!"
